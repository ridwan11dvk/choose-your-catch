Why Can't I Catch This Fish?
Stop guessing. Pick a fish, and the game tells you exactly why it will — or won't — bite right now.




🎣 What it does
Ever stood by the water wondering why a fish just won't show up? Open a simple in-game menu, pick any fish item — vanilla or modded — and instantly see whether you can catch it right now.
If you can't, it tells you in plain language exactly what's stopping you — no more tabbing out to the wiki.

This mod is built for players, not mod authors, so it focuses on readable answers instead of dumping raw internal data at you.

✨ Features

    Works with vanilla and many modded fish — including fish added through standard

    Data/Fish

    and

    Data/Locations

    rules.
    Instant "catchable now" check — a green dot marks fish you can catch at this very moment.
    Plain-language reasons — wrong season, time, weather, location, fishing level, rod, Magic Bait, catch limits, legendary "once only", and special spawn rules — all explained like a helper, not
    a debug log.
    Smart search — find fish by name, item ID, location, or even which mod added them.
    Knows where each fish came from — "Vanilla fish" or "Added by the <Mod> mod", shown directly in the report.
    Reads special conditions — translates many internal rule queries into readable sentences (for example season, weather, special order, or date-based requirements).
    Player-first UI — blockers shown first, helpful tips next, details last.
    Legacy-friendly — also handles many vanilla fish that still rely on older location tokens from

    Data/Fish


🚀 Getting started

    Install SMAPI (4.0.0+).
    Unzip this mod into your Stardew Valley/Mods folder.
    Load a save and press Left Shift + H to open the inspector.
    Click a fish on the left, then read why it can or can't be caught on the right.

⚙️ Configuration
Install Generic Mod Config Menu (optional) to change settings in-game:

    Open fish inspector — rebind the hotkey (default: Left Shift + H).
    Show only fish items — keeps the list focused on actual fish items instead of non-fish spawn entries.

🔌 Compatibility

    Stardew Valley 1.6.0+, SMAPI 4.0.0+.
    Safe to add or remove at any time — it only reads your game data. It does not edit saves or change fishing behavior.
    Multiplayer-safe (the inspector UI is client-side).
    Designed to work with vanilla fish and many fish-adding mods that use standard Stardew 1.6 data assets.

Important note:

    This mod is intentionally fish items only. Non-fish entries added by other mods — such as weapon-type rewards or other special items — will not appear in the inspector.
    Some heavily custom or opaque modded conditions may still fall back to a more generic explanation instead of a perfect sentence.
